Telangana

Telangana, the 29th state of India, was formed on 2 June 2014.
Known for its rich history, vibrant culture, historical monuments
and rapid development, Telangana as a land of endless
opportunities and warm hospitality.

Capital: Hyderabad

Official Language: Telugu

Formation: 2 June 2014

Location: South-Central India

Historical Journey

  • Ancient region with history over 2,000 years.
  • Ruled by Satavahanas, Kakatiyas, Bahmani,
    Qutub Shahis Nizams.
  • Warangal Fort and Ramappa Temple built during
    Kakatiya era.
  • Golconda Fort was a center for diamond trade under
    Qutub Shahis.
  • Hyderabad State integrated into India in 1948
    (Operation Polo).
  • Merged with Andhra State in 1956.
  • Telangana became 29th state of India on
    2 June 2014.

Cultural Heritage

Telangana's culture is a beautiful blend of traditions, folk arts and a unique way of life.

  • Folk Dances: Perini,Lambadi,Bathukamma,Dance
  • Traditional Attire: Pochampally Saress,Pancha
  • Festivals,Folk Music,Handicrafts, and more
  • Warm hospitality and vibrant traditions
